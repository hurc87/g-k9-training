Are you getting a new puppy?

We offer a puppy program to help you and your puppy get off to the best start. In the program, we offer advice on supplies you should get before your puppy comes home, to make sure you are well equipped to enable your puppy to settle into his or her new home from day 1. We will then help you build a good foundation through a mixture of crate training, puppy manners, engagement and early socialization - done the right way! The team are here to guide you each step of the way, with weekly homework and milestone check ins for you and your puppy.

I have had a passion for dogs from a young age, my parents are champion show dog breeders, so I have been around dogs and the industry my entire life.
